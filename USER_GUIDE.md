# FIIT User Guide

Welcome to FIIT, your execution coach. FIIT removes thinking friction by turning vague goals into a clear daily plan.

## Quick Start

1) **Start**
   - Tell FIIT the goal you want to make real.
   - Keep it messy. Clarity comes next.

2) **Clarify**
   - Answer one focused question at a time.
   - Each answer reduces ambiguity and locks a decision.

3) **Commit**
   - FIIT generates your 7‑day plan.
   - Tap **Commit Plan** to lock it.

4) **Today Mode**
   - See only 1–3 tasks for today.
   - Tap **Start** to complete the next action.

## If You’re Stuck

- When you defer a task a few times, FIIT offers a **5‑minute version**.
- Tap **Make this easier** to get a micro‑step you can do right now.

## Resetting Your Plan

- Plans are locked after you commit.
- You can reset your plan with **Reset Plan** (limited on the free tier).
- Coach Mode unlocks unlimited resets.

## Coach Mode (Paid)

Coach Mode unlocks:
- Unlimited plan resets
- Unlimited micro‑step rewrites
- Execution audits and deeper coaching
